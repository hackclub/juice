# Undead Courier

**Team Members:** Hridya Agrawal

**How would you pitch your game in one line?**
A post-apocalyptic FPS survival adventure where you battle relentless zombie bosses and harness their rare essences to craft a world-saving potion.

**What is the key mechanic(s) that make it fun?**
Diverse Boss Encounters: Face off against uniquely designed zombie bosses, each dropping a vital essence.
Dynamic Movement: Engage in fluid, responsive player movement that keeps combat exciting and unpredictable.

**How does the game actually play? (Core Game Loop)**
Exploration & Combat: Roam a deep, eerie forest and take on waves of regular zombies, sharpening your survival instincts.
Boss Battles: Strategically confront and defeat formidable zombie bosses to secure their precious essences.

Narrative Progression: Deliver your crafted potion to a key scientist, unraveling the mystery and saving the world.


**How will you keep players engaged for 30 minutes? What's the general scope of your game (will change as you go)?**
Intense Combat and Variety: A rich arsenal of guns and combat scenarios keeps the adrenaline pumping for 30 minutes and beyond.
Expansive Content: Enjoy a blend of main missions and engaging side quests that deepen the lore and provide additional challenges.
